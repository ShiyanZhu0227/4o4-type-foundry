Cirkit
Information
Cirkit is a variable font built around playful geometry and elastic motion. Its key ideas come from a stretchable stem and a circle module that can shift and reshape themselves, letting the type move and breathe with whatever layout it’s in. Cirkit is rooted in intuitive design and takes cues from the clean pathways of circuit boards, mixed with a bit of Art Deco elegance. It ends up feeling both engineered and expressive, with plenty of personality in between. Thanks to its flexible axes, Cirkit can go from neat and structured to open and energetic, giving designers a fun, responsive tool to play with.
